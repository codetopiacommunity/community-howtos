# Discord Guide

## What is Discord?

Discord is a free communication platform built around communities. Think of
it as a place where people with shared interests gather to chat, learn, and
collaborate. It is organized into servers and the Codetopia Community has
its own server where all the action happens.

---
## Step 1: Download and set up Discord

We recommend downloading Discord on both your phone and your computer.
This way you are never cut off from the community, whether you are at
your desk or on the go.

Discord has an official guide that walks you through downloading the app,
creating your account, and getting set up. Follow it carefully, then come
back here to continue.

👉 [Discord Sign Up and Registration Guide](https://support.discord.com/hc/en-us/articles/31676852332439-Discord-Sign-Up-and-Registration-Guide)

> **Once you are done, come back here and continue with Step 2.**
